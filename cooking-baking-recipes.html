Chocolate Cake

60 mins 8 servings Dessert

A rich, moist chocolate cake that's perfect for any celebration.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla; beat for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin).
  5. Pour into prepared pans and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Cool completely before frosting.

Vanilla Cupcakes

40 mins 12 servings Dessert

Light and fluffy vanilla cupcakes topped with creamy buttercream frosting.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • For frosting: 1 cup butter, 3 cups powdered sugar, 2 tsp vanilla, 2 tbsp milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a 12-cup muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  5. Gradually beat in flour mixture alternating with milk.
  6. Divide batter among cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full.
  7. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  8. Cool completely before frosting with buttercream.

Marble Cake

70 mins 10 servings Dessert

A beautiful swirled cake combining vanilla and chocolate batters.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup hot water

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  5. Gradually mix in the flour mixture alternating with milk.
  6. Divide batter in half. Mix cocoa powder and hot water, then stir into one half of the batter.
  7. Spoon batters alternately into the prepared pan. Swirl with a knife for marble effect.
  8. Bake for 50-60 minutes until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  9. Cool in pan for 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.

Tiramisu

30 mins + 4 hrs chilling 8 servings Dessert

Classic Italian dessert with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cream.

Ingredients

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 1/4 cups heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pound mascarpone cheese
  • 1/4 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 2 tablespoons rum
  • 24 ladyfinger cookies
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, whisk together egg yolks and sugar until well blended.
  2. Whisk in milk and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture boils.
  3. Boil gently for 1 minute, then remove from heat and allow to cool slightly.
  4. Cover tightly and chill in refrigerator for 1 hour.
  5. In a medium bowl, beat cream with vanilla until stiff peaks form.
  6. Whisk mascarpone into the yolk mixture until smooth.
  7. Fold in whipped cream. Set aside.
  8. In a small bowl, combine coffee and rum.
  9. Split ladyfingers in half lengthwise and drizzle with coffee mixture.
  10. Arrange half of soaked ladyfingers in bottom of a 7x11 inch dish.
  11. Spread half of mascarpone mixture over ladyfingers, then repeat layers.
  12. Sprinkle with cocoa powder. Cover and refrigerate for 4-6 hours, until set.

Chicken Curry

45 mins 4 servings Main Dish

A flavorful and aromatic chicken curry that's perfect with rice or naan.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add garlic and ginger, cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add spices and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  5. Add chicken and cook until browned on all sides.
  6. Add tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Pour in coconut milk, bring to a simmer.
  8. Cover and cook for 20-25 minutes until chicken is tender.
  9. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice.

Chicken Pasta

35 mins 4 servings Main Dish

Creamy chicken pasta with garlic, parmesan, and fresh herbs.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Add chicken and cook until golden and cooked through, about 6-8 minutes. Remove and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add garlic and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  6. Add chicken broth, heavy cream, Italian seasoning, and red pepper flakes.
  7. Bring to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes until slightly thickened.
  8. Stir in parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  9. Return chicken to the skillet and add cooked pasta. Toss to coat.
  10. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve immediately.

Butter Chicken

50 mins 4 servings Main Dish

Rich and creamy Indian butter chicken with tender chicken pieces in a tomato-based sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs boneless chicken thighs, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 1 can (14 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, 1 tsp garam masala, cumin, and turmeric.
  2. Add chicken pieces and marinate for at least 30 minutes (or overnight).
  3. Heat oil in a large pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook until browned, about 5 minutes. Remove and set aside.
  5. In the same pan, add onion and cook until soft, about 3 minutes.
  6. Add garlic and ginger, cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  7. Stir in tomato sauce, remaining garam masala, and bring to a simmer.
  8. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
  9. Stir in cream, butter, and brown sugar. Return chicken to the pan.
  10. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until chicken is cooked through and sauce thickens.
  11. Season with salt to taste and garnish with fresh cilantro.
  12. Serve with naan bread or rice.

Masala Dosa

40 mins + overnight soaking 4 servings Main Dish

South Indian crispy crepes filled with spiced potato filling.

Ingredients

  • For Dosa Batter:
  • 2 cups rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • For Potato Filling:
  • 4 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp chopped cilantro
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. For Dosa Batter:
  2. Wash and soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 4-6 hours.
  3. Drain and grind to a smooth batter with water as needed.
  4. Mix the batters together, add salt, and ferment overnight.
  5. For Potato Filling:
  6. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds.
  7. When they splutter, add onions, green chilies, and curry leaves. Sauté until onions are translucent.
  8. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  9. Add mashed potatoes and mix thoroughly. Cook for 5 minutes.
  10. Garnish with cilantro and set aside.
  11. To Make Dosa:
  12. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle. Pour a ladleful of batter and spread in a circular motion.
  13. Drizzle oil around the edges and cook until golden brown.
  14. Place a portion of potato filling in the center and fold the dosa.
  15. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.

Garlic Naan

30 mins + 2 hrs rising 8 servings Side Dish

Soft and fluffy Indian flatbread flavored with garlic and butter.

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, mix warm water with sugar and yeast. Let sit for 10 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, salt, and baking powder.
  3. Add the yeast mixture, yogurt, and oil to the flour. Mix to form a soft dough.
  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5-6 minutes until smooth.
  5. Place in an oiled bowl, cover, and let rise for 2 hours until doubled in size.
  6. Punch down the dough and divide into 8 equal portions.
  7. Roll each portion into an oval shape about 1/4 inch thick.
  8. Heat a cast iron skillet over high heat.
  9. Place the rolled dough on the hot skillet and cook until bubbles form.
  10. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown spots appear.
  11. Brush with garlic butter and sprinkle with cilantro.
  12. Serve hot with curry or dips.

Chapati

30 mins 8 servings Side Dish

Simple and healthy whole wheat Indian flatbread.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 3/4 cup warm water (approximately)
  • Extra flour for dusting
  • Ghee or butter for brushing (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour and salt.
  2. Add oil and mix with your fingertips until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
  3. Gradually add warm water and knead to form a soft, pliable dough.
  4. Cover with a damp cloth and let rest for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Divide the dough into 8 equal portions and form into balls.
  6. Roll each ball in dry flour and roll out into a thin circle (about 6-7 inches in diameter).
  7. Heat a flat pan or tawa over medium-high heat.
  8. Place the rolled chapati on the hot pan.
  9. When small bubbles start to appear, flip the chapati.
  10. Press gently with a cloth or spatula. The chapati should puff up.
  11. Remove from heat and brush with ghee or butter if desired.
  12. Serve hot with curry, dal, or vegetable dishes.

Roast Potatoes

45 mins 6 servings Side Dish

Crispy on the outside, fluffy on the inside roasted potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs potatoes (Russet or Yukon Gold)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp paprika

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Peel potatoes and cut into 1.5-inch chunks.
  3. Place potatoes in a large pot and cover with cold water.
  4. Add 1 tsp salt to the water and bring to a boil.
  5. Boil for 5-7 minutes until edges are softened but centers are still firm.
  6. Drain potatoes and let them steam dry for 5 minutes.
  7. Shake the potatoes in the colander to roughen the edges.
  8. In a large bowl, mix olive oil, garlic, herbs, salt, pepper, and paprika.
  9. Add potatoes to the bowl and toss to coat evenly.
  10. Spread potatoes on a baking sheet in a single layer.
  11. Roast for 30-35 minutes, turning halfway through, until golden and crispy.
  12. Serve hot as a side dish.

Papadams

15 mins 8 servings Side Dish

Crispy, thin Indian lentil wafers that make a perfect accompaniment to any curry.

Ingredients

  • 8 store-bought papadams
  • Oil for deep frying (or cooking spray for microwave method)
  • Optional spices for homemade papadams:
  • 2 cups urad dal flour
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Water as needed

Instructions

  1. For Store-Bought Papadams:
  2. Method 1 - Deep Frying:
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan to 375°F (190°C).
  4. Carefully slide one papadam into the hot oil.
  5. It will immediately puff up and become crisp (about 5 seconds).
  6. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.
  7. Repeat with remaining papadams.
  8. Method 2 - Microwave:
  9. Lightly spray both sides of papadam with cooking spray.
  10. Place on a microwave-safe plate.
  11. Microwave on high for 30-45 seconds until it puffs up and becomes crisp.
  12. For Homemade Papadams (requires drying time):
  13. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.
  14. Add oil and mix well.
  15. Gradually add water to form a stiff dough.
  16. Divide into small balls and roll into thin circles.
  17. Dry in the sun for 2-3 days until completely dry.
  18. Cook using either method above.
